Characters in Attack on Titan

Main Characters

  • Eren Yeager
    Eren is a determined and passionate young man who becomes a key player in humanity's fight against the Titans. He is driven by a burning desire for freedom and justice.
  • Mikasa Ackerman
    Mikasa is Eren's adoptive sister and a highly skilled soldier known for her incredible combat abilities. She is fiercely protective of Eren and is driven by a desire to keep him safe.
  • Armin Arlert
    Armin is a strategic thinker and a close friend of Eren and Mikasa, playing a crucial role in devising plans against the Titans. He values intelligence and uses his wits to outsmart his opponents.
